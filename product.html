Handwoven Storage Baskets (Set of Two)

A timeless storage essential designed to bring warmth and order to your space. Handwoven using natural jute, these baskets are sturdy yet soft in form—ideal for everyday storage without compromising on aesthetics.

Use them to store blankets, cushions, laundry, toys, or everyday clutter—beautifully concealed in plain sight.

Why it’s special

  • Handwoven from 100% natural jute
  • Structured yet flexible form
  • Leather loop handles for easy movement
  • Neutral tone that blends seamlessly into any interior

Best for

Living rooms, bedrooms, wardrobes, shelves

Material & Care

100% natural jute with leather handles. Wipe clean with a dry or lightly damp cloth. Avoid prolonged exposure to moisture.

Dimensions

Large basket: Approx. 14” diameter × 12” height
Small basket: Approx. 12” diameter × 10” height

Thoughtfully designed and handmade using natural materials. Minor variations in texture and weave are part of the craft and make each piece unique.
