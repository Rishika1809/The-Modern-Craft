Our Story

Founded in 2024, we started with a simple belief: the things we hold should have a soul.

The Artisan Way


In a world of mass production, we chose to slow down. Every basket, planter, and shelf is crafted by hand in our small studio. We don't just make objects; we preserve the tradition of weaving and woodworking that has been passed down for generations.
